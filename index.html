Kanatal

Experience Beyond luxury

Kanatal located in Uttarakhand at 2590 Mtr altitude and 75 Kms from Dehradun Airport and 40 Kms from Mussoorie and five hrs drive from Delhi, very scenic setting surrounding by mountains and Jungle, with breathtaking views and scenery. Kanatal starts from Kaudia range and goes up to Saklana Range, still unexplored hence is intact with nature’s beauty.

One can do trekking or take a walk into Kaudia Jungle, a dense forest area to explore and capture the beauty. One can also spot few wild animals by taking a jungle safari ride. Must visit Tehri Dam for various water sports and many natural springs that are quite prevalent in the region.
